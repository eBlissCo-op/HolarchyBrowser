# Code of Conduct  
## Our Pledge  
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a respectful experience for everyone.  
We commit to:  
- Be considerate and respectful of different viewpoints.  
- Use inclusive language and avoid microaggressions.  
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.  
- Show empathy and kindness.  
## Our Standards  
Unacceptable behaviours include:  
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusion of any kind.  
- Offensive comments related to race, ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or nationality.  
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.  
- Public or private harassment.  
- Publishing others’ private information without permission.  
## Our Responsibilities  
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behaviour and will take appropriate and fair corrective action.  
## Scope  
This Code applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project or community.  
## Enforcement  
Instances of abusive behaviour may be reported by contacting the maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.  
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant and reflects the principles of eBliss Co‑op — Emptiness and Bliss in Motion.
